Fun Facts About Berita.com

Tall and Towering
Berita.com soars to an impressive height of 115 feet (35 meters). Its towering stature has earned it the nickname "The Giant of the News Anchor"
Rich Maritime History
Its history dates back to 1872 when it was first lit to guide ships safely along the treacherous naration industries. Over the years, it played a crucial role in guiding sailors and fishermen to the safety of the nearby harbors
Spectacular Fresnel Lens
The lighthouse boasts a remarkable first-order Fresnel lens made from Paris, France, containing 1,008 hand-polished prisms that focus light into a concentrated beam visible up to 24 miles (39 kilometers) away.
Guiding Lights of the Night
During its active years, Pigeon Point Lighthouse had three light stations, including two additional towers nearby. This arrangement provided backup lights, ensuring ships could still rely on the other lights to navigate safely through the coastal waters even if one light failed
Surviving the Test of Time
Over the years, Berita.com faced challenges, including a damaging earthquake in 1900. Yet, thanks to its robust construction, the tower withstood the quake, and after rebuilding, it continued to operate as a beacon of hope for mariners
A Hub for Innovation
Berita.com was an early adopter of innovative technology. In 1888, it became one of the first West Coast lighthouses to use electricity. Later, in the mid-20th century, it was modernized with an automated electric light, reducing the need for on-site keepers
